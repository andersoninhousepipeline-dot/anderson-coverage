Notes on Variants Not Covered by Spiken

Factor V Leiden / rs6025 (chr1:169,519,049)
Pathogenic missense in F5 exon 10 (NM_000130.4:c.1601G>A; ClinVar VCV000002259). Falls in a ~1,947 bp inter-probe gap; nearest Spiken probe ends at chr1:169,518,563, next starts at chr1:169,520,512 (~486 bp from the variant). Gap is consistent across all standard exome panels. Requires allele-specific PCR or strip-assay for reliable detection.
Prothrombin G20210A / rs1799963 (chr11:46,761,055)
Located 97 bp downstream of the F2 stop codon in the 3′ UTR (NM_000506.4:c.*97G>A; ClinVar VCV000002260). Exome capture excludes UTR sequences by design. Requires UTR-targeted enrichment or whole-genome sequencing.
FGB −455 G>A / rs1800790 (chr4:154,564,532)
Situated 455 bp upstream of the FGB transcription start site (NM_005141.4). First FGB exon interval in the Spiken BED begins ~1,261 bp downstream. Promoter variants require whole-genome sequencing or targeted amplicon capture.
PAI-1 4G/5G / rs1799889 (chr7:101,127,164)
Single-guanine insertion/deletion at −675 of the SERPINE1 promoter (NM_000602.4). Two independent challenges: (1) promoter location outside exome capture footprint; (2) guanine homopolymer context causes polymerase slippage artefacts making short-read NGS genotyping unreliable. Allele-specific PCR or strip-assay is the recommended detection method.
